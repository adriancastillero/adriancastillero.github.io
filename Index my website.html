ABOUT THE U.A.E

By: Adrian Castillero 10D

The main history

The UAE was established in 1971 when the seven Sheikhs of each emirate came together and created the United Arab Emirates. The UAE has seven emirates and each one has one sheikh, which is the ruler. The seven Emirates of the UAE are: Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Ajman, Sharjah, Umm Al Quwain, Al Ain, Fujairah. The UAE has a total population of 9.4 million and its capital is Abu Dhabi. The total area of the UAE is 83,000 km² and it shares borders with Qatar, Saudi Arabia and Oman. The UAE is an active leading country in the field of clean and renewable energy worldwide. Its immense efforts culminated by choosing Abu Dhabi to host the headquarters of the International Renewable Energy (Irena)

The Laws of the UAE

The UAE have many laws that are very strict in order to make the country a safer place and a better place to live

The UAE is a country with many laws and regulations and even though some people might not like these laws it is all for the better of the country, so that it can become a better and safer place for both single expats and families. Some of these strict laws are: No swearing in public, no fireworks, checking someones phone is also illegal and it also comes with a fine
